Help Center

Find answers to your questions below or contact our support team.

How do I deposit funds?

You can deposit funds by clicking on the Deposit button from your wallet dashboard and following the instructions.

How can I transfer money to another wallet?

Simply navigate to the Transfer page, enter the recipient's details, and input the amount you wish to send.

What are the transaction limits?

Transaction limits vary by your verification level. Please refer to your account limits page for details.
